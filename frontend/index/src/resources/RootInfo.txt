## Shortkeys
Start/Finish editing file: `Ctrl + Shift + E`
Uppercase of selected text: `Ctrl + Shift + U`
Lowercase of selected text: `Ctrl + Shift + L`
Remove line under cursor: `Ctrl + Shift + X`
Wrap selected text with code block: `Ctrl + Shift + \``
Remove line breaks of selected text: `Ctrl + Shift + R`
Duplicate line: `Ctrl + Shift + D`
Add 4-space-indents: `Tab`
